How AI Cry Detection Works

Our proprietary neural network analyzes spectral features of infant vocalizations through a multi-stage clinical pipeline.

Acoustic Logic

Extracts fundamental frequencies and MFCC coefficients from raw audio streams.

Pattern Mapping

Compares signatures against our database of 50,000+ validated pediatric recordings.

Diagnostic Scans

Classifies the cry into clinical buckets (Hungry, Pain, Respiratory distress).

Real-time Alerting

Immediate visual and audio notification push through the monitoring dashboard.
