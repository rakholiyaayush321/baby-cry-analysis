Spectral Mapping

Our models convert raw audio streams into Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCCs). We capture the micro-frequencies and temporal shifts in cries that human ears cannot decipher, comparing them against thousands of clinically validated acoustic markers.
